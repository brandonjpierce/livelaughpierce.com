Story

Corinne and Brandon had known of each other since highschool through common friends but had never met before. Until... on July 4th, 2013 at a bar called Mulligans, Corinne approached Brandon to say hello. They caught up like they were old friends and went on with their night separately. It was not until much later that Corinne realized they had never met before that night. Time goes on and their friend Casey found out they had spoken to one another and decided to play matchmaker and set them up on a date. The rest is history!

Corinne and Brandon began officially dating on August 1st, 2013. After four years of dating they knew they wanted to get married so they decided to get engaged!
